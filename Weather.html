"PAGASA issues El Niño watch"














MANILA — State Weather Bureau PAGASA has issued an El Niño watch following observations from model forecasts showing an increased probability of occurrence by July, August and September this year. An El Niño watch is issued when there is at least a 55 percent chance of the phenomenon developing within the next six months. PAGASA Senior Weather Specialist Dr. Joseph Basconcillo, in a press conference on Wednesday, explained that the El Niño watch is a forecast and does not mean it is already happening. “Sa mga susunod na buwan lalo na June, July and August, meron nang 62 percent chance that El Niño is likely to emerge and last at least until the end of 2026,” he said.
