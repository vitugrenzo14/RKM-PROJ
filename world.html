"Eyes specialized seminar for Filipinos migrating to Germany"














The Commission on Filipinos Overseas (CFO) is mulling a specialized Pre-Departure Orientation Seminar (PDOS) for migrants bound for Germany. In a statement, CFO said they are in talks with the Goethe-Institut (GI) for pre-migration and cultural integration programs to ensure that Filipinos who would move to Germany are well-prepared. “Successful migration is the foundation of effective diaspora engagement… When our emigrants are well-integrated, they are better positioned to maintain strong economic, cultural, and political ties with the motherland,” said CFO Secretary Dante Ang II. CFO’s move to strengthen the pre-departure program for Filipinos bound for Germany aims to ensure that they and their national identity remain protected despite the major move.
