"2 Esports Tournament finals Headline ‘Call of Duty: Mobile’ Summer Fest 2026"














The heat is on in the battlefield as Call of Duty: Mobile (CODM) began its 2026 summer season on Monday, March 30. From now until May 10, players can score up to 3,500 COD points (CP) in-game by logging in and completing missions, global online games developer Garena announced. The main event of the new season, however, is in the two tournaments to be hosted in Manila in April and May. From April 6 to 12, users can register for Garena Elite Philippines (GEP) 2026, where a total of US$40,000 is up for grabs. It will be the game developer’s first LAN event that to have both Multiplayer and Battle Royale formats. More tournament details and registration will be available in-game, with a live Grand Final scheduled for May 23.
