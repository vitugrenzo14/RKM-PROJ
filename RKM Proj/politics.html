"Comelec en banc impeachment sought"














MANILA — A group of lawyers wants the impeachment of the Commission on Elections en banc for alleged betrayal of public trust, other high crimes, and violations of the Republic Act 8436 or the automated election system law. The lawyers, Marvin Aceron and Sikini Labastilla, were joined by Robby Kwan Laurel, who introduced himself as a social entrepreneur, in a press conference at the Club Filipino, Monday morning. The group admitted no congressman has so far endorsed the complaint, which is a constitutional requirement before the House of Representatives can even entertain it. The complaint has 4 charges.
