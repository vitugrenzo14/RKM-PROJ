"Basyang leaves impassable roads, power outages in Mindanao"














MANILA — Several road sections and bridges remain impassable, while power has yet to be restored in parts of Mindanao following the onslaught of Typhoon Basyang, latest report from the disaster office showed. In a situational report as of 8 p.m. Sunday, the National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (NDRRMC) said 37 roads and one bridge in Northern Mindanao remain impassable, along with three roads and two bridges in the Caraga region. Meanwhile, at least five cities and municipalities in Caraga continue to experience power interruptions. The Philippine Ports Authority said trips remain cancelled in Albay, Palawan, Leyte, and Eastern Samar due to strong to gale-force winds.
