OIL PRICES JUMP The Philippines has seen the price
of fuel hit historic highs since the US-Israeli war with
Iran forced the partial closure of the Strait of Hormuz,
with President Ferdinand Marcos most recently saying
stocks could last until June 30. The skyrocketing cost
of diesel and gasoline has driven protests by groups
representing the country's jeepney drivers and others.
