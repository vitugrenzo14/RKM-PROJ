"The Tourism Paradox: PH leads tourism GDP but lags in foreign arrivals"














MANILA — Despite having the largest tourism economy in the ASEAN region, the Philippines continues to trail its neighbors in terms of actual tourist arrivals, particularly when compared to Thailand and Vietnam. “The World Travel and Tourism Council (WTTC) in their latest economic impact report delivered during the ASEAN Tourism Forum has attested to the fact that the Philippines is now the biggest tourism economy in the ASEAN,” Department of Tourism Secretary Christina Frasco said in a media forum on Wednesday. According to the WTTC report, the Philippines ranked first in ASEAN, with tourism contributing USD 91.8 billion to the country’s GDP and generating millions of jobs, accounting for 23 percent of total national employment.
